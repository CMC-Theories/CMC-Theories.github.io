Chase Craig

My name is Chase Craig, I am a senior at University of Oregon. I will be graduating soon with a B.S. majoring in computer and information science, mathematics and physics. I have used this time to perform research in both a quantum optics lab and a physiology lab.
For the former, I am in charge of designing and programming controllers for CVD (chemical vapor deposition) and AFM (atomic force microscopy), along with building a Michelson Interferometer and smaller programs to generate geometry given a set of constraints.
For the latter, I am in charge of developing and maintaining a code base for analysing EEG (electroencephalogram) data and extract charactistics from it. I am also exploring using Artificial Intelligence to classify patients based on this data.
